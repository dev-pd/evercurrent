# ECO-178 — Chassis Bracket Material Change

## Summary

Change chassis bracket BRK-A1 from AL-6063-T5 to AL-7075-T6 in two
high-flux regions to restore thermal margin. Effective on rev C
drawings. Applies to all DVT and PVT builds.

## Rationale

During DVT thermal cycling unit 7 exceeded the 85 C chassis spec at
the bracket boss face, peaking at 91 C. FEA confirmed the boss face
was conduction-limited under worst-case load. AL-7075-T6 has a
26 percent higher yield strength, allowing thinner web sections that
shed heat faster, and a thermal conductivity acceptable for the BMS
proximity zone.

## Affected parts

| Part   | Old material  | New material  |
|--------|---------------|---------------|
| BRK-A1 | AL-6063-T5    | AL-7075-T6    |
| BRK-A2 | AL-6063-T5    | AL-6063-T5    |

Only BRK-A1 is changed. BRK-A2 is downstream of the heat spreader and
not thermally limited.

## Downstream impact

- BOM rev C must be updated to the new alloy line before procurement
  releases the next batch.
- The BMS thermal model assumes AL-6063 and must be re-run before DVT
  exit.
- PrecisionCoat is qualified for AL-7075 finishing.

## Risks tracked

- AlumWest has not been through our PPAP for AL-7075-T6. If we ship
  before PPAP is complete we are out of process.
- ExtruCo strike could extend into the next sprint and reduce AlumWest
  capacity for combined extrusion plus finishing.
